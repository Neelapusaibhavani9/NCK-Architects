    Building Plans

    • Architectural Services
    • Residential & Commercial

    NCK Architects provides professional architectural building plans for residential and commercial construction projects in Visakhapatnam. Our building plans are designed with precision, functionality, and compliance with local authority regulations.

    We focus on optimal space utilization, structural safety, ventilation planning, and modern design standards. Every plan is customized based on client requirements, site conditions, and VMRDA or municipality approval norms.

    Our team ensures accurate layout planning, structural coordination, and complete documentation support required for building approvals. We deliver detailed drawings that simplify execution for contractors and engineers.

    • Customized residential and commercial building plans
    • Floor planning with optimal space utilization
    • Approval drawings for VMRDA & Municipality
    • Structural coordination and execution support

    From independent houses to commercial buildings, we ensure professional planning that balances aesthetics, functionality, and regulatory compliance.
