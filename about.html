    About NCK Architects

    Professional Architectural & Engineering Consultancy in Visakhapatnam

    NCK Architects is a multidisciplinary firm specializing in architectural design, interior design, structural engineering, land surveying, and building approvals. We deliver complete residential and commercial project solutions with a strong focus on functionality, structural safety, and modern aesthetics. Our experienced team ensures precision planning, regulatory compliance, and professional execution from concept to completion.
    • Complete building plans and architectural design solutions
    • Modern residential and commercial interior design services

    • VMRDA and municipality building approval assistance
